New to Aromatherapy?

Aromatherapy

The term “aromatherapy” was coined by a French perfumer and chemist René-Maurice Gattefossé in a book he wrote on the topic that was published in 1937. Aromatherapy works through the sense of smell and skin absorption using products such as these: diffusers, inhalers, body oils, facial steamers and others.

Ingredients

Not 97%, not 99%, 100% natural. When we say natural, we actually mean it. We prioritize pure and clean ingredients above all else. We believe in nature and its ability to soothe ailments, calm the mind and invoke feelings of joy—that’s why we make plant-based products to power every aspect of your life.

Benefits

Each essential oil has an array of unique healing properties, uses, and effects. Combining essential oils to create a synergistic blend creates even more benefits. Incense and Fragrances also help to increase calm and focus, aid sleep, purify your space and allow you the simple please of enjoying a lovely scent.
